Reservation Policy

Reservation Hours

We accept dinner reservations between 5:00 PM and 10:00 PM, Tuesday through Sunday. We're closed on Mondays.

Late Arrival

We hold reservations for 15 minutes past the reservation time. Please call if you're running late.

Cancellation

If you need to cancel, please do so at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a cancellation fee.

Large Parties

For groups of 8 or more, please contact us directly at 041 234 5678 or email reservations@whiperingwillow.com.
